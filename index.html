Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

Is Nandi really better than Aashirvaad?

Nandi is the #1 Best Seller on Blinkit in Atta with a 4.5-star rating across 25,000+ reviews, ahead of national brands in our home markets. We use 100% Sharbati wheat from MP and grind it daily in our FSSC 22000 certified facility, so the atta you receive is fresher than what travels from larger cross-country supply chains. Read the side-by-side on our Nandi vs Aashirvaad deep dive.

Why is a UP-based brand #1 on Blinkit?

UP is India's largest wheat-producing state, so we source closer to the field than any national brand. Our Prayagraj facility grinds and packs daily, then ships to Blinkit dark stores within hours, which is why Nandi consistently ranks #1 in Prayagraj, Lucknow, Kanpur, Varanasi and Gorakhpur on Blinkit, Zepto and Instamart.

Where can I buy Nandi products?

Nandi products are available on Blinkit, Zepto, Swiggy Instamart, BigBasket, and 25,000+ retail stores across Uttar Pradesh. Quick delivery available in most pin codes.

What is the shelf life of Nandi products?

Flours (Atta, Maida, Sooji, Besan): 6 months. Spices: 12 months. Pickles: 12 months. Salt: 24 months. Sattu: 6 months. Always check the packaging for the exact best-before date.

Are Nandi products 100% vegetarian?

Yes. Every Nandi product is 100% vegetarian, including our Meat Masala (Vegetarian Spice Blend). None of our products contain any animal-derived ingredients.

How can I become a Nandi distributor?

Visit our Distributor Network page or contact us at info@ecavo.in. We are expanding across UP and looking for partners in every district.

Can I order Nandi products in bulk?

Yes. For bulk and institutional orders (hotels, restaurants, caterers, hostels), reach out to us at info@ecavo.in or WhatsApp +91 81888 54505 (Tej Bali).

What certifications does Nandi/Ecavo have?

We hold FSSAI, FSSC 22000 (international food safety), MSME, IEC (import-export), and ODOP (One District One Product) certifications. Details on our Quality & Trust page.
